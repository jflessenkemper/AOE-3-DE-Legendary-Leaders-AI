Legendary Leaders AI

A standalone Age of Empires III: Definitive Edition mod that combines the base civilizations with the playable revolution roster.
Each nation is mapped to a themed leader personality and a clear battlefield identity.

Standard Nations (22)

  • Fortress: Doubles down on infantry warbands, flooding the battlefield with foot soldiers and coyote cavalry. No artillery at all.
